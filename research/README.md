# Market Research

This directory contains market research and analysis materials including:

- Market size and opportunity analysis
- Competitive landscape research
- Customer discovery and validation
- Industry trends and insights
- Target market segmentation

## Structure

- `market-analysis/` - Market size, trends, and opportunity assessment
- `competitive-analysis/` - Competitor research and positioning
- `customer-research/` - Customer interviews, surveys, and personas
- `industry-reports/` - Third-party research and industry reports
