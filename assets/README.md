# Marketing Assets

This directory contains marketing and sales assets including:

- Brand guidelines and logos
- Marketing collateral and materials
- Sales presentations and decks
- Website assets and content
- Social media assets

## Structure

- `brand/` - Brand guidelines, logos, and identity assets
- `collateral/` - Brochures, one-pagers, and marketing materials
- `presentations/` - Sales decks and presentation materials
- `digital/` - Website assets, banners, and digital materials
- `social/` - Social media assets and content
- `templates/` - Reusable templates and frameworks
