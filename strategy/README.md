# GTM Strategy

This directory contains go-to-market strategy documents and planning materials including:

- Product positioning and messaging
- Pricing strategy
- Sales and marketing strategies
- Channel partner strategies
- Launch planning and execution

## Structure

- `positioning/` - Product positioning and value proposition
- `pricing/` - Pricing models and strategy
- `sales/` - Sales strategy and processes
- `marketing/` - Marketing strategy and campaigns
- `partnerships/` - Channel and strategic partnerships
- `launch/` - Product launch planning and execution
